Blocked Chain Design Page

Features

  • each listener listens to one marketplace for events
  • each listener contains one character of the key mixed in with eleven random characters
  • a master key contains all the characters that key is not except for three characters
  • the marketplace has a minimum price per course of $300
  • each listener gets paid per key they give

Marketplace

  • max price of $1,000
  • min price of $300
  • affiliates gets paid half of the cost
  • fully decentralized
  • keep mapping of products and their details
  • board of directors approves courses to be sold into marketplace
  • board of directors gets paid up to $36/hour and .2% of each course that sells
  • other marketplaces can be made

Listener

  • uses node.js
  • listens to one marketplace
  • contains one character of the key mied in with eleven random characters
  • longest running nodes become master nodes
